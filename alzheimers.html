Overview

We redesigned AD risk reports and study materials to help cognitively normal older adults accurately interpret 5-year risk and trust results.

My role

  • Study design (measures, tasks); IRB-aligned materials
  • Prototype variants: icon arrays, bar/dot charts, FAQ, clinician script
  • Analysis: comprehension, recall, worry, clarity

Process

  1. Baseline evaluation of legacy report
  2. Lo-fi → hi-fi prototypes; cognitive walkthroughs
  3. Usability sessions + short A/B visualization/wording tests

Key findings

  • Icon arrays ↑ comprehension & recall vs numeric-only
  • “Memory and thinking problems” phrasing reduced confusion
  • Adding “next steps” increased usefulness & trust
